# FM Instrument Banks and Volume Models

For MIDI-style formats (MIDI, XMI, MUS, HMP/HMI), the instrument bank determines how every instrument sounds. Different banks are optimized for different games and sound systems.

## Bank Tag Meanings

- **:4op:** - Uses 4-operator FM synthesis (richer sound)
- **:p4op:** - Partial 4-operator support
- **:NON-GM:** - Non-General MIDI, may have unusual instrument mappings
- **:MISS-INS:** - Some instruments may be missing
